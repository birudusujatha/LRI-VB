About The Conference

Host Institution

Godavari Global University (GGU) has a rich legacy, originating from the Godavari Institute of Engineering & Technology (GIET) into a premier educational institution. Today, GGU serves over 10,000 students from 15 Indian states and 13 countries, establishing itself as a distinguished center for learning in Andhra Pradesh.

GGU is NAAC A++ accredited (3.58/4) and has been consistently ranked in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). The university is also recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under 2(f) & 12(B), reinforcing its commitment to academic excellence and research.

The university offers a diverse range of programs through its four specialized schools: the School of Computing, School of Engineering, School of Sciences, School of Pharmacy & Health Sciences.

The International Conference on AI/ML Digital Transformation & Sectoral Innovations (ICAI25) is designed to serve as a global hub for fostering knowledge exchange, collaboration, and innovation in the rapidly evolving fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Digital Transformation.

ICAI25 will bring together a diverse audience of researchers, industry experts, policymakers, and innovators to share insights, present cutting-edge research, and discuss real-world applications of AI and ML. Through keynote speeches, technical sessions, panel discussions, and workshops, participants will gain access to the latest trends, methodologies, and best practices in the field.

The conference will showcase groundbreaking research, case studies, and success stories that demonstrate how AI and ML are transforming industries. From startups to established enterprises, participants will gain insights into how to leverage these technologies to create innovative solutions, optimize processes, and unlock new opportunities.
