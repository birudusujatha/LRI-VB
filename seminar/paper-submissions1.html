Submission Guidelines

Paper Format

  • All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference.
  • Papers should be submitted in PDF format.
  • Full papers should be 6-8 pages in length, including figures, tables, and references.
  • Use the standard IEEE conference template for formatting your paper.
  • All papers will undergo a double-blind peer-review process.

Topics of Interest

Papers are invited in any of the 8 conference tracks:

For detailed track information and subtopics, please visit the Tracks page.

Ethical Guidelines

  • Authors are expected to adhere to the highest standards of research integrity and ethics.
  • Plagiarism in any form will lead to immediate rejection of the paper.
  • All submissions will be checked using plagiarism detection software.
  • If the research involves human subjects, appropriate ethical clearances should be mentioned in the paper.

Submission Process

1

Prepare Your Paper

Format your paper according to the IEEE conference template. Ensure that your paper adheres to the page limit and formatting guidelines.

2

Fill the Submission Form

Complete the online submission form with your paper details, author information, and track selection.

3

Upload Your Paper

Upload your paper in PDF format (max 10MB). You will receive a confirmation with a submission ID upon successful upload.

4

Review Process

Your paper will undergo a double-blind peer-review process. The program committee will assess the technical quality, relevance, and originality of your submission.

5

Notification

Authors will be notified of the acceptance or rejection of their papers by May 15, 2025.

6

Camera-Ready Submission

If your paper is accepted, you will need to submit the camera-ready version by June 1, 2025, incorporating any changes based on reviewer feedback.

7

Registration

At least one author of each accepted paper must register for the conference and present the paper.

Publication

All accepted and presented papers will be published in the conference proceedings. The organizing committee is in the process of arranging for the publication of selected high-quality papers in reputed international journals (subject to additional review).

Copyright Information

Authors of accepted papers will be required to sign a copyright transfer form. The copyright of the paper will be transferred to the publisher of the conference proceedings.
